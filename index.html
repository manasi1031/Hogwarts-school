About Us

History

Hogwarts was founded in the 10th century by Godric Gryffindor, Rowena Ravenclaw, Helga Hufflepuff and Salazar Slytherin. The school was established in the highlands of Scotland to educate young witches and wizards in a safe environment away from prosecution. Theory has it that the name was derived from a dream by Rowena which showed a warty hog leading her to a cliff by a lake. Since then, Hogwarts has been educating most wizarding community in the United Kingdom and surrounding areas. The schools actual location is hidden from other wizarding schools and muggles.

Addmission & Entry

Hogwarts is a co-educational and boarding secondary school, taking children from the ages of eleven to eighteen. Admission in Hogwarts is selective and only those children that show an affinity towards magic would be selected to attend. Main mode of transportation to the school is via Hogwarts Express train (fictional)that students taken from platform 9 3/4 at KIngs Cross, London. Students board the train there and then get down in Hogsmeade station near Hogwarts. There they are met by Hagrid - Keeper of Keys, Game and Grounds; who then takes the kids via boat / carriages to school. This is the same process for collecting students back from London too.
